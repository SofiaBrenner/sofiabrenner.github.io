About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at University of Kassel, working in the Discrete Mathematics group led by Torsten Mütze. From 2022 to 2024, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the group of Pascal Schweitzer at TU Darmstadt. From 2019 to 2022, I was a PhD student of Burkhard Külshammer at Friedrich Schiller University Jena.

From October 2025 to March 2026, I am at Charles University in Prague, Czech Republic, to work on my project "Extending Automorphisms of Structures: Interactions of group theory and model theory", funded by the German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD). My advisor in Prague is Jan Hubicka.

I am excited to start as an assistant professor at University of Leipzig in April 2026.

I am interested in research topics at the intersection of group theory and combinatorics. During my PhD, I worked in representation theory of finite groups and symmetric algebras. As a postdoctoral researcher, I became interested in topics in combinatorics and algebraic graph theory. I also work on projects in theoretical computer science.
